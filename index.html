Awesome Features

Ballup has plenty of features to help you locate the nearest pick-up game!

Multi-Device Functionality

Supports iOS and Android.

Easy Setup

With the integration of location capabilities, finding local games and players is easy. Simply fill out the profile details, check-off game preferences, and choose from a wide selection of matches.

Map View

This allows for the viewing of local games being played in the surrounding area. The map displays the sport’s icon and by clicking it the game details will be displayed.

Social Media

This app is social media friendly with the integration of popular outlets such as Facebook, Twitter, and Skype. This allows users to post game scores, pictures, and details with friends and family. This app will also take information from friends to facilitate player matching.

Specifications

There are several customizable options for a large variety of game preferences. Key options include: age-range, sports type, and game duration.

Select the privacy of your game!


Public:

Open your game up to anyone. Choose a maximum capacity of players, and your game’s sport, location, time, and current players will be displayed to anyone in the area until your capacity is reached.



Private:

Decide who gets to play in your game. Display your game’s sport and time to players in the area, but players must request to play with you. You’ll be able to see their skill rating in the sport and their sportsmanship rating based on the reviews of previous players. You can accept or deny anyone you’d like. Only accepted players will be given a location.



Invitational:

Personally invite the people you know to your game. Only they will see the details of the game and will notify you if they are coming.









Create your own Player Card!


Experience:

Includes the amount of games played and average amount of time spent for each pick up game. Depending on the amount of experience, players can be evaluated and matched by the automatic pick up game generator.



Reputation:

Includes a rating system accessible by teammates and opposing teammates. Having the rating sender invisible by other users, this feature allows for a better evaluation of the group of people playing a sport. The rating system depends on sportsmanship, friendliness, and overall behavior.



Player Statistics:

Includes player statistics such as goals, points, assists, fouls, cards, and shots. This feature would help the matching process of players for potential games. This feature could also aid fellow players evaluate a game depending on the skill specifications of participating members.











Locate the nearest pick-up games in map view!


Overview:

Detect your location. Shows the location of public sports and some private sports open for you. Sports type icon displayed in the map. Get detail number of players and time after clicking.



Sport/Privacy Specification:

On the right corner of map, the button provided for adding your own game. Select sports type, choose privacy of your name, edit sports information, send invitation and show in the map.



Easy Functionality:

Upon clicking on the logo in the bottom left, the user returns to the main menu.
